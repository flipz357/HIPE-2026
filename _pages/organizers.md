## HIPE-2026 Organizing Team

The HIPE-2026 lab is organized by an interdisciplinary team with expertise in natural language processing, digital humanities, historical text mining, and shared task coordination:

- Juri Opitz — University of Zurich, Switzerland  
  Postdoctoral researcher with a focus on semantic representation, evaluation, and generative models.

- Maud Ehrmann — EPFL, Switzerland  
  Co-initiator of the HIPE evaluation series; expert in information extraction, historical NLP, and digital humanities.

- Simon Clematide — University of Zurich, Switzerland  
  Experienced in multilingual NLP, named entity processing, and evaluation campaign organization.

- Matteo Romanello — University of Zurich (SARI), Switzerland  
  Specialist in citation mining, computational literary studies, and historical document annotation.

- Emanuela Boros — EPFL, Switzerland  
  Postdoctoral researcher working on relation and event extraction in historical sources.

- Andrianos Michail — University of Zurich, Switzerland  
  PhD researcher with top-performing shared task submissions and extensive mentoring experience.

## Partner Projects and Data Providers

- Impresso — _Media Monitoring of the Past_  
  A research initiative dedicated to large-scale semantic enrichment of digitized historical newspapers.
